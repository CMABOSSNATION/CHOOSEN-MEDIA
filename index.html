Who We Are

More Than a Print Shop

Chosen Media (U) Ltd is Uganda's dedicated book printing partner — built for institutions, educators, and authors who refuse to compromise on quality.

We combine precision printing technology with deep understanding of the Ugandan education market. Whether you need a P.6 Social Studies textbook or an A-Level ICT Handbook, we deliver materials that inspire students and stand up to daily classroom use.

Every project is handled with care — from cover design consultation to binding and delivery. Our team at Karobwa Tower, Nkrumah Road, is ready to turn your manuscript into a finished, professional publication.
